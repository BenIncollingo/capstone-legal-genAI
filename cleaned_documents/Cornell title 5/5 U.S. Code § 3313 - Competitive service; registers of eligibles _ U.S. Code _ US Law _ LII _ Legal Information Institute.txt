§ 3313 - Competitive
service; registers of eligibles
The names of applicants who have qualified in examinations for the competitive
service shall be entered on appropriate registers or lists of eligibles in the
following order—
(1) for scientific and professional positions in GS–9 or higher, in the order of their
ratings, including points added under section 3309 of this title; and
(2) for all other positions—
