5 U.S. Code 

§ 3314 - Registers;
preference eligibles who resigned
A preference eligible who resigns, on request to the Office of Personnel
Management, is entitled to have his name placed again on all registers for which he
may have been qualified, in the order named by section 3313 of this title.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 420; Pub. L. 95–454, title IX, 

§ 906(a)(2), Oct. 13,
1978, 92 Stat. 1224.)
