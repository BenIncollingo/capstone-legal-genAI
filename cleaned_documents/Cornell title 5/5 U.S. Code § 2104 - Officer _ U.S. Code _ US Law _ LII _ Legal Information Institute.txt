5 U.S. Code 

§ 2104 - Officer
(a) For the purpose of this title, “officer”, except as otherwise provided by this
section or when specifically modified, means a justice or judge of the United States
and an individual who is—
(1) required by law to be appointed in the civil service by one of the following
acting in an official capacity—
(A) the President;
(B) a court of the United States;
(C) the head of an Executive agency; or
