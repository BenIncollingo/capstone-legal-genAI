§ 5724b - Taxes on
reimbursements for travel,
transportation, and relocation expenses
(a) Under regulations prescribed under section 5738 of this title and to the extent
considered necessary and appropriate, as provided therein, appropriations or
other funds available to an agency for administrative expenses are available for
the reimbursement of substantially all of the Federal, State, and local income taxes
incurred by an individual, or by an individual and such individual’s spouse (if filing
jointly), for any travel, transportation, or relocation expenses furnished in kind, or
