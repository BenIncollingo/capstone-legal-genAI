5 U.S. Code 

§ 2903 - Oath; authority to
administer
(a) The oath of office required by section 3331 of this title may be administered
by an individual authorized by the laws of the United States or local law to
administer oaths in the State, District, or territory or possession of the United
States where the oath is administered.
(b) An employee of an Executive agency designated in writing by the head of the
Executive agency, or the Secretary of a military department with respect to an
employee of his department, may administer—
