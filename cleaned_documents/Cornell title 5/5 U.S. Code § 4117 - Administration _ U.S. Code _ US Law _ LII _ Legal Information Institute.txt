5 U.S. Code 

§ 4117 - Administration
The Office of Personnel Management has the responsibility and authority for
effective promotion and coordination of the training programs under this chapter and
training operations thereunder. The functions, duties, and responsibilities of the Office
under this chapter are subject to supervision and control by the President and review
by Congress.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 438; Pub. L. 95–454, title IX, 

§ 906(a)(2), (3), Oct.
13, 1978, 92 Stat. 1224.)
