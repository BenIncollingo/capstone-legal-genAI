5 U.S. Code 

§ 3309 - Preference
eligibles; examinations; additional
points for
A preference eligible who receives a passing grade in an examination for
entrance into the competitive service is entitled to additional points above his
earned rating, as follows—
(1) a preference eligible under section 2108(3)(C)–(G) of this title—10 points; and
(2) a preference eligible under section 2108(3)(A)–(B) of this title—5 points.
