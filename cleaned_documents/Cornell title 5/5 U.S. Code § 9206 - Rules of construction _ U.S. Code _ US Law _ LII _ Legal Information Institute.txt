5 U.S. Code 

§ 9206 - Rules of
construction
Nothing in this chapter may be construed to—
(1) authorize any officer or employee of an agency to request the disclosure of
information described under subparagraphs (B) and (C) of section 9201(4); or
(2) create a private right of action for any person.
(Added Pub. L. 116–92, div. A, title XI, 

§ 1122(a), Dec. 20, 2019, 133 Stat. 1607.)
