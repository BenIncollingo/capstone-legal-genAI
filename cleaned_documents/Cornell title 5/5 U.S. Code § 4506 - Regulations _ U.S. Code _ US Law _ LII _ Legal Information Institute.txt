§ 4506 - Regulations
The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations and instructions
under which the awards programs set forth by this subchapter shall be carried out.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 443; Pub. L. 95–454, title V, 

§ 503(d), Oct. 13,
1978, 92 Stat. 1184; Pub. L. 97–35, title XVII, 

§ 1703(b)(2), Aug. 13, 1981, 95 Stat. 756;
Pub. L. 107–67, title VI, 

§ 641(b), Nov. 12, 2001, 115 Stat. 555.)
