5 U.S. Code 

§ 3362 - Promotion; effect
of incentive award
An agency, in qualifying and selecting an employee for promotion, shall give due
weight to an incentive award under chapter 45 of this title. For the purpose of this
section, “agency” and “employee” have the meanings given them by section 4501 of
this title.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 426.)
