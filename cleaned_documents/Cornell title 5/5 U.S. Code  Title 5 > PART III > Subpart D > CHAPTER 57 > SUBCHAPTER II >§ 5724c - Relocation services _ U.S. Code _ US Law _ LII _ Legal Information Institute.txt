Under regulations prescribed under section 5738 of this title, each agency may
enter into contracts to provide relocation services to agencies and employees for the
purpose of carrying out this subchapter. An agency may pay a fee for such services.
Such services include arranging for the purchase of a transferred employee’s
residence.
