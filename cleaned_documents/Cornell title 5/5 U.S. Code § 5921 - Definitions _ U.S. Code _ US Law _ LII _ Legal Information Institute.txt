5 U.S. Code 

§ 5921 - Definitions
For the purpose of this subchapter—
(1) “Government” means the Government of the United States;
(2) “agency” means an Executive agency and the Library of Congress, but does
not include a Government controlled corporation;
(3) “employee” means an employee in or under an agency and more specifically
defined by regulations prescribed by the President;
(4) “United States”, when used in a geographical sense, means the several States
and the District of Columbia;
