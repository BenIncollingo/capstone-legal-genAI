5 U.S. Code 

§ 2905 - Oath; renewal
(a) An employee of an Executive agency or an individual employed by the
government of the District of Columbia who, on original appointment, subscribed
to the oath of office required by section 3331 of this title is not required to renew
the oath because of a change in status so long as his service is continuous in the
agency in which he is employed, unless, in the opinion of the head of the Executive
agency, the Secretary of a military department with respect to an employee of his
department, or the Commissioners of the District of Columbia, the public interest
so requires.
