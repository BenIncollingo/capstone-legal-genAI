5 U.S. Code 

§ 3324 - Appointments to
positions classified above GS–15
(a) An appointment to a position classified above GS–15 pursuant to section 5108
may be made only on approval of the qualifications of the proposed appointee by
the Director of the Office of Personnel Management on the basis of qualification
standards developed by the agency involved in accordance with criteria specified
in regulations prescribed by the Director. This section does not apply to a position
—
(1) to which appointment is made by the Chief Judge of the United States Tax
Court;
