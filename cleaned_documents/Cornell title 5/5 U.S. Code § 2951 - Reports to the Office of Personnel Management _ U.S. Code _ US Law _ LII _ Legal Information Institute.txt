5 U.S. Code 

§ 2951 - Reports to the
Office of Personnel Management
The President may prescribe rules which shall provide, as nearly as conditions of
good administration warrant, that—
(1) the appointing authority notify the Office of Personnel Management in writing
of the following actions and their dates as to each individual selected for
appointment in the competitive service from among those who have been
examined—
(A) appointment and residence of appointee;
