5 U.S. Code 

§ 5706 - Allowable travel
expenses
Except as otherwise permitted by this subchapter or by statutes relating to
members of the uniformed services, only actual and necessary travel expenses may
be allowed to an individual holding employment or appointment under the United
States.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 500.)
