5 U.S. Code 

§ 3408 - Employee
organization representation
If an employee organization has been accorded exclusive recognition with respect to
a unit within an agency, then the employee organization shall be entitled to represent
all employees within that unit employed on a part-time career employment basis.
(Added Pub. L. 95–437, 

§ 3(a), Oct. 10, 1978, 92 Stat. 1058, 

§ 3398; renumbered 

§ 3408,
Pub. L. 95–454, title IX, 

§ 906(c)(1)(B), Oct. 13, 1978, 92 Stat. 1226.)
