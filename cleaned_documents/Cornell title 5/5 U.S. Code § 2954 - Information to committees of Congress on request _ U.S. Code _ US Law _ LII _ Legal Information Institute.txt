5 U.S. Code 

§ 2954 - Information to
committees of Congress on request
An Executive agency, on request of the Committee on Government Operations of
the House of Representatives, or of any seven members thereof, or on request of the
Committee on Governmental Affairs of the Senate, or any five members thereof, shall
submit any information requested of it relating to any matter within the jurisdiction of
the committee.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 413; Pub. L. 103–437, 

§ 3(b), Nov. 2, 1994, 108
Stat. 4581.)
