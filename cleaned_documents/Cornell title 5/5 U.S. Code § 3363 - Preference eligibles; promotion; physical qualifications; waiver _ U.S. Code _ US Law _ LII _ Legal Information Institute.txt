§ 3363 - Preference
eligibles; promotion; physical
qualifications; waiver
In determining qualifications of a preference eligible for promotion to another
position in the competitive service, an Executive agency, or the government of
the District of Columbia, the Office of Personnel Management or other examining
agency shall waive—
(1) requirements as to age, height, and weight, unless the requirement is
essential to the performance of the duties of the position; and
