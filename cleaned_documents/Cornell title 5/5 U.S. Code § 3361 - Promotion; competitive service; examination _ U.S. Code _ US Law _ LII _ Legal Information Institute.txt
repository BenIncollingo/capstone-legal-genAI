5 U.S. Code 

§ 3361 - Promotion;
competitive service; examination
An individual may be promoted in the competitive service only if he has passed an
examination or is specifically excepted from examination under section 3302 of this
title. This section does not take from the President any authority conferred by section
3301 of this title that is consistent with the provisions of this title governing the
competitive service.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 426.)
