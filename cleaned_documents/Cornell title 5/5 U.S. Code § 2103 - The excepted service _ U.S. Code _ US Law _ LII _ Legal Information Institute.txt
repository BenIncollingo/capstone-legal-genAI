5 U.S. Code 

§ 2103 - The excepted
service
(a) For the purpose of this title, the “excepted service” consists of those civil
service positions which are not in the competitive service or the Senior Executive
Service.
(b) As used in other Acts of Congress, “unclassified civil service” or “unclassified
service” means the “excepted service”.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 408; Pub. L. 95–454, title IV, 

§ 401(c), Oct. 13,
1978, 92 Stat. 1154.)
