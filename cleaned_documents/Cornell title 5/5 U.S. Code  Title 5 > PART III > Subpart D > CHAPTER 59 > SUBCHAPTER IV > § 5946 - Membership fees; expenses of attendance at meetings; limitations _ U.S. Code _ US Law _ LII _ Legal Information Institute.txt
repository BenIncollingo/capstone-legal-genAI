§ 5946 - Membership fees;
expenses of attendance at meetings;
limitations
Except as authorized by a specific appropriation, by express terms in a general
appropriation, or by sections 4109 and 4110 of this title, appropriated funds may
not be used for payment of—
(1) membership fees or dues of an employee as defined by section 2105 of this
title or an individual employed by the government of the District of Columbia in a
society or association; or
