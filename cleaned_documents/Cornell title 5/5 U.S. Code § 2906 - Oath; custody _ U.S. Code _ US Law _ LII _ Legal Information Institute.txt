5 U.S. Code 

§ 2906 - Oath; custody
The oath of office taken by an individual under section 3331 of this title shall be
delivered by him to, and preserved by, the House of Congress, agency, or court to
which the office pertains.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 412.)
