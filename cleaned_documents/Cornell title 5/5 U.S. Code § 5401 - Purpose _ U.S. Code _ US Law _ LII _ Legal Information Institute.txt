5 U.S. Code 

§ 5401 - Purpose
The purpose of this chapter is to promote, through the creation of a Human Capital
Performance Fund, greater performance in the Federal Government. Monies from the
Fund will be used to reward agencies’ highest performing and most valuable
employees. This Fund will offer Federal managers a new tool to recognize employee
performance that is critical to the achievement of agency missions.
(Added Pub. L. 108–136, div. A, title XI, 

§ 1129(a), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1642.)
