The Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation may, under regulations
prescribed by the Director, pay a cash award of up to 10 percent of basic pay to any
Bureau employee who maintains proficiency in a language or languages critical to the
mission or who uses one or more foreign languages in the performance of official
duties.
(Added Pub. L. 111–117, div. B, title II, 

§ 219(a), Dec. 16, 2009, 123 Stat. 3141.)
