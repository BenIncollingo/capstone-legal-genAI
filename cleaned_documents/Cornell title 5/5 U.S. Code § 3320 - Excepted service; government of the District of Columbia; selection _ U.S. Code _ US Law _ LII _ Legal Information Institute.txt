5 U.S. Code 

§ 3320 - Excepted service;
government of the District of Columbia;
selection
The nominating or appointing authority shall select for appointment to each vacancy
in the excepted service in the executive branch and in the government of the District
of Columbia from the qualified applicants in the same manner and under the same
conditions required for the competitive service by sections 3308–3318 of this title. This
section does not apply to an appointment required by Congress to be confirmed by, or
made with the advice and consent of, the Senate.
