5 U.S. Code 

§ 5709 - Air evacuation
patients: furnished subsistence
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, and under regulations prescribed under
section 5707 of this title, an employee and his dependents may be furnished
subsistence without charge while being evacuated as a patient by military aircraft of
the United States.
(Added Pub. L. 91–481, 

§ 1(1), Oct. 21, 1970, 84 Stat. 1081.)
