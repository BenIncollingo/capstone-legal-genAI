§ 2902 - Commission;
where recorded
(a) Except as provided by subsections (b) and (c) of this section, the Secretary of
State shall make out and record, and affix the seal of the United States to, the
commission of an officer appointed by the President. The seal of the United States
may not be affixed to the commission before the commission has been signed by
the President.

the control of the Secretary of Agriculture, the Secretary of Commerce, the
