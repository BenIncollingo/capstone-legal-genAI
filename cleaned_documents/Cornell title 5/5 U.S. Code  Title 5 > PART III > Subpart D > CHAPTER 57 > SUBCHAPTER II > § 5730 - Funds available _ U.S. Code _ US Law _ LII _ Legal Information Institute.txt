5 U.S. Code 

§ 5730 - Funds available
Funds available for travel expenses of an employee are available for expenses of
transportation of his immediate family, and funds available for transportation of
things are available for transportation of household goods and personal effects, as
authorized by this subchapter.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 506.)
