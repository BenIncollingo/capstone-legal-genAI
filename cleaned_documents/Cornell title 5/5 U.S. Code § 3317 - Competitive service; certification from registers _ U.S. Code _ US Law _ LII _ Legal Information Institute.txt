§ 3317 - Competitive
service; certification from registers
(a) The Office of Personnel Management shall certify enough names from the top
of the appropriate register to permit a nominating or appointing authority who
has requested a certificate of eligibles to consider at least three names for
appointment to each vacancy in the competitive service.
(b) When an appointing authority, for reasons considered sufficient by the Office,
has three times considered and passed over a preference eligible who was
