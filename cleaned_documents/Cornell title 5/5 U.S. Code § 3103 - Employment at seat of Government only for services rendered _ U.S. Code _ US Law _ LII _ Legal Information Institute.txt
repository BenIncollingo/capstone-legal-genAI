5 U.S. Code 

§ 3103 - Employment at
seat of Government only for services
rendered
An individual may be employed in the civil service in an Executive department at the
seat of Government only for services actually rendered in connection with and for the
purposes of the appropriation from which he is paid. An individual who violates this
section shall be removed from the service.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 415.)
