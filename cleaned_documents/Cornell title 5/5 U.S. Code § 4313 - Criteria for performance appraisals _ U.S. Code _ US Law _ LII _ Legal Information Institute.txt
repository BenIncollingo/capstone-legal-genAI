5 U.S. Code 

§ 4313 - Criteria for
performance appraisals
Appraisals of performance in the Senior Executive Service shall be based on both
individual and organizational performance, taking into account such factors as—
(1) improvements in efficiency, productivity, and quality of work or service,
including any significant reduction in paperwork;
(2) cost efficiency;
(3) timeliness of performance;
