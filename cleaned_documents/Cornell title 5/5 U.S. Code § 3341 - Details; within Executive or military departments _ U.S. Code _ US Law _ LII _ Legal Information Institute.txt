5 U.S. Code 

§ 3341 - Details; within
Executive or military departments
(a) The head of an Executive department or military department may detail
employees among the bureaus and offices of his department, except employees
who are required by law to be exclusively engaged on some specific work.
(b)
(1) Details under subsection (a) of this section may be made only by written
order of the head of the department, and may be for not more than 120 days.
