5 U.S. Code 

§ 5942a - Separate
maintenance allowance for duty at
Johnston Island
(a) Notwithstanding section 5536 of this title, and under regulations prescribed
by the President, an employee of an Executive agency (other than a Government
corporation) who is assigned to a post of duty at Johnston Island, a possession of
the United States in the Pacific Ocean, is entitled to receive a separate
maintenance allowance if the head of the employing agency finds that—
(1) it is necessary for the employee to maintain the employee’s spouse or
dependents, or both, at a location other than Johnston Island—

(B) for the convenience of the Federal Government; and
(2) the allowance is needed to help the employee meet the additional

both, at such other location rather than at the post.
(b) The regulations prescribed by the President shall include provisions for
determining the rate at which an allowance under this section shall be paid.
