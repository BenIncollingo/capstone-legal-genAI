5 U.S. Code 

§ 3113 - Restriction on
reemployment after conviction of
certain crimes
An employee shall be separated from service and barred from reemployment in
the Federal service, if—
(1) the employee is convicted of a violation of section 201(b) of title 18; and
(2) such violation related to conduct prohibited under section 1010(a) of the
Controlled Substances Import and Export Act (21 U.S.C. 960(a)).
