5 U.S. Code 

§ 3112 - Disabled
veterans; noncompetitive appointment
Under such regulations as the Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe, an
agency may make a noncompetitive appointment leading to conversion to career or
career-conditional employment of a disabled veteran who has a compensable service-
connected disability of 30 percent or more.
(Added Pub. L. 95–454, title III, 

§ 307(b)(1), Oct. 13, 1978, 92 Stat. 1147.)
