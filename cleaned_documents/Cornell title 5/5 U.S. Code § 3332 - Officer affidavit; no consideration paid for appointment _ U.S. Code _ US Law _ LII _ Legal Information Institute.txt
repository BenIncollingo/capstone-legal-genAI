5 U.S. Code 

§ 3332 - Officer affidavit;
no consideration paid for appointment
An officer, within 30 days after the effective date of his appointment, shall file with
the oath of office required by section 3331 of this title an affidavit that neither he nor
anyone acting in his behalf has given, transferred, promised, or paid any consideration
for or in the expectation or hope of receiving assistance in securing the appointment.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 424.)
