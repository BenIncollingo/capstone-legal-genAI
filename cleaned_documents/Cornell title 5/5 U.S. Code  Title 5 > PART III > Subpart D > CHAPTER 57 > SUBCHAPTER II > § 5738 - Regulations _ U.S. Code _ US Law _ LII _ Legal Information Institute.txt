5 U.S. Code 

§ 5738 - Regulations
(a)
(1) Except as specifically provided in this subchapter, the Administrator of
General Services shall prescribe regulations necessary for the administration
of this subchapter.
(2) The Administrator of General Services shall include in the regulations
authority for the head of an agency or his designee to waive any limitation of
this subchapter or in any implementing regulation for any employee relocating
to or from a remote or isolated location who would suffer hardship if the
limitation were not waived. A waiver of a limitation under authority provided in
