5 U.S. Code 

§ 3311 - Preference
eligibles; examinations; crediting
experience
In examinations for the competitive service in which experience is an element of
qualification, a preference eligible is entitled to credit—
(1) for service in the armed forces when his employment in a similar vocation to
that for which examined was interrupted by the service; and
(2) for all experience material to the position for which examined, including
experience gained in religious, civic, welfare, service, and organizational activities,
