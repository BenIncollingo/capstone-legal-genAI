5 U.S. Code 

§ 5752 - Travel expenses of
Senior Executive Service candidates
Employing agencies may pay candidates for Senior Executive Service positions travel
expenses incurred incident to preemployment interviews requested by the employing
agency.
(Added Pub. L. 95–454, title IV, 

§ 409(b), Oct. 13, 1978, 92 Stat. 1173.)
