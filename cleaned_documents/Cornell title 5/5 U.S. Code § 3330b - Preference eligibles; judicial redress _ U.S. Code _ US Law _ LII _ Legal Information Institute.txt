§ 3330b - Preference
eligibles; judicial redress
(a) In lieu of continuing the administrative redress procedure provided under
section 3330a(d), a preference eligible, or a veteran described by section 3330a(a)
(1)(B) with respect to a violation described by such section, may elect, in
accordance with this section, to terminate those administrative proceedings and
file an action with the appropriate United States district court not later than 60
days after the date of the election.
(b) An election under this section may not be made—
