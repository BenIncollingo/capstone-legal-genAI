5 U.S. Code 

§ 3310 - Preference
eligibles; examinations; guards,
elevator operators, messengers, and
custodians
In examinations for positions of guards, elevator operators, messengers, and
custodians in the competitive service (other than for positions of housekeeping aides
in the Department of Veterans Affairs), competition is restricted to preference eligibles
as long as preference eligibles are available.
