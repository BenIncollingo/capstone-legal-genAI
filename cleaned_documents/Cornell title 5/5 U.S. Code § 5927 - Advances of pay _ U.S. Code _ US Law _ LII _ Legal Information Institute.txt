5 U.S. Code 

§ 5927 - Advances of pay
(a) Up to three months’ pay may be paid in advance—
(1) to an employee upon the assignment of the employee to a post in a
foreign area;
(2) to an employee, other than an employee appointed under section 303 of
the Foreign Service Act of 1980 (and employed under section 311 of such Act),
who—
(A) is a citizen of the United States;

(C) requires (or has a family member who requires) medical treatment

(3) to an employee compensated pursuant to section 408 of the Foreign
Service Act of 1980, who—
(A) pursuant to United States Government authorization is located

(B) requires medical treatment outside the country of employment in
circumstances specified by the President in regulations.
(b) For the purpose of this section, the term “country of employment”, as used
with respect to an individual under subsection (a)(3), means the country (or other
area) outside the United States where such individual is hired (as described in
subsection (a)(3)) by the Government.
