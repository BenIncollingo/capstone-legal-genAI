5 U.S. Code 

§ 4121 - Specific training
programs
In consultation with the Office of Personnel Management, the head of each
agency shall establish—
(1) a comprehensive management succession program to provide training to
employees to develop managers for the agency; and
(2) a program to provide training to managers on actions, options, and strategies
a manager may use in—
(A) relating to employees with unacceptable performance;
