5 U.S. Code 

§ 3107 - Employment of
publicity experts; restrictions
Appropriated funds may not be used to pay a publicity expert unless specifically
appropriated for that purpose.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 416.)
