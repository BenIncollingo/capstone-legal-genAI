§ 4702 - Research
programs
The Office of Personnel Management shall—
(1) establish and maintain (and assist in the establishment and maintenance of)
research programs to study improved methods and technologies in Federal
personnel management;
(2) evaluate the research programs established under paragraph (1) of this
section;
(3) establish and maintain a program for the collection and public dissemination
of information relating to personnel management research and for encouraging
