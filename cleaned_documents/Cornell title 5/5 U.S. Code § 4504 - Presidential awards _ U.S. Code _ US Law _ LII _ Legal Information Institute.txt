5 U.S. Code 

§ 4504 - Presidential
awards
The President may pay a cash award to, and incur necessary expense for the
honorary recognition of, an employee who—
(1) by his suggestion, invention, superior accomplishment, or other personal
effort contributes to the efficiency, economy, or other improvement of
Government operations or achieves a significant reduction in paperwork; or
(2) performs an exceptionally meritorious special act or service in the public
interest in connection with or related to his official employment.
