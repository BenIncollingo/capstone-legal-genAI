§ 4119 - Training for
employees under the Office of the
Architect of the Capitol and the Botanic
Garden
(a) The Architect of the Capitol may, by regulation, make applicable such
provisions of this chapter as the Architect determines necessary to provide for
training of (1) individuals employed under the Office of the Architect of the Capitol
and the Botanic Garden and (2) other congressional employees who are subject to
the administrative control of the Architect. The regulations shall provide for
