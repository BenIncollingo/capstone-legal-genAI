§ 5942 - Allowance based
on duty at remote worksites
(a) Notwithstanding section 5536 of this title, an employee of an Executive
department or an independent establishment who is assigned to duty, except
temporary duty, at a site so remote from the nearest established communities or
suitable places of residence as to require an appreciable degree of expense,
hardship, and inconvenience, beyond that normally encountered in metropolitan
commuting, on the part of the employee in commuting to and from his residence
and such worksite, is entitled, in addition to pay otherwise due him, to an
allowance of not to exceed $10 a day. The allowance shall be paid under
regulations prescribed by the President establishing the rates at which the
