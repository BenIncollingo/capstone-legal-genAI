5 U.S. Code 

§ 3136 - Regulations
The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations to carry out the
purpose of this subchapter.
(Added Pub. L. 95–454, title IV, 

§ 402(a), Oct. 13, 1978, 92 Stat. 1160.)
