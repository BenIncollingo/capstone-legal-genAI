5 U.S. Code 

§ 3385 - Effect on other
authority
This subchapter shall not limit the authority of the Secretary to reassign temporarily
an air traffic controller to other duties with or without notice, in the interest of the safe
or efficient separation and control of air traffic or the physical or mental health of a
controller; or to reassign permanently or separate a controller under any other
provision of law.
(Added Pub. L. 92–297, 

§ 3(a), May 16, 1972, 86 Stat. 143; amended Pub. L. 96–347,


§ 1(b), Sept. 12, 1980, 94 Stat. 1150.)
