§ 5925 - Post differentials
(a) A post differential may be granted on the basis of conditions of environment
which differ substantially from conditions of environment in the continental
United States and warrant additional pay as a recruitment and retention incentive.
A post differential may be granted to an employee officially stationed in the United
States who is on extended detail in a foreign area. A post differential under this
subsection may not exceed 35 percent of the rate of basic pay.
(b) Any employee granted a differential under subsection (a) of this section may
be granted an additional differential for an assignment to a post determined to
have especially adverse conditions of environment which warrant additional pay
