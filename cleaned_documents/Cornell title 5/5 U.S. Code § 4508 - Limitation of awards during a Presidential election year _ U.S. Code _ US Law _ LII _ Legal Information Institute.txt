5 U.S. Code 

§ 4508 - Limitation of
awards during a Presidential election
year
(a) For purposes of this section, the term—
(1) “Presidential election period” means any period beginning on June 1 in a
calendar year in which the popular election of the President occurs, and
ending on January 20 following the date of such election; and
(2) “senior politically appointed officer” means any officer who during a
Presidential election period serves—
