5 U.S. Code 

§ 4110 - Expenses of
attendance at meetings
Appropriations available to an agency for travel expenses are available for expenses
of attendance at meetings which are concerned with the functions or activities for
which the appropriation is made or which will contribute to improved conduct,
supervision, or management of the functions or activities.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 436.)
