5 U.S. Code 

§ 3597 - Reemployment
following limited appointment in the
Foreign Service
An employee of any agency who accepts, with the consent of the head of that
agency, a limited appointment in the Foreign Service under section 309 of the Foreign
Service Act of 1980 is entitled, upon the expiration of that appointment, to be
reemployed in that employee’s former position or in a corresponding or higher
position in that agency. Upon reemployment under this section, an employee shall be
entitled to any within-grade increases in pay which the employee would have received
if the employee had remained in the former position in the agency.
