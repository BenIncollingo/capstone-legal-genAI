5 U.S. Code 

§ 3327 - Civil service
employment information
(a) The Office of Personnel Management shall provide that information
concerning opportunities to participate in competitive examinations conducted by,
or under authority delegated by, the Office of Personnel Management shall be
made available to the employment offices of the United States Employment
Service.
(b) Subject to such regulations as the Office may issue, each agency shall
promptly notify the Office and the employment offices of the United States
Employment Service of—
