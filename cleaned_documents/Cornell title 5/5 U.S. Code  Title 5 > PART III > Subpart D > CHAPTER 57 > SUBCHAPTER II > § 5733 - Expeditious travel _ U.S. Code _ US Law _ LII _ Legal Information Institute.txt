5 U.S. Code 

§ 5733 - Expeditious travel
The travel of an employee shall be by the most expeditious means of transportation
practicable and shall be commensurate with the nature and purpose of the duties of
the employee requiring such travel.
(Added Pub. L. 90–206, title II, 

§ 222(c)(1), Dec. 16, 1967, 81 Stat. 641.)
