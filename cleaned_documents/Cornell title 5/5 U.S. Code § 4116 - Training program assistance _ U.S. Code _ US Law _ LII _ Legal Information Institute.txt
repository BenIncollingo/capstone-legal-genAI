5 U.S. Code 

§ 4116 - Training program
assistance
The Office of Personnel Management, on request of an agency, shall advise and
assist in the establishment, operation, and maintenance of the training programs and
plans of the agency under this chapter, to the extent of its facilities and personnel
available for that purpose.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 438; Pub. L. 95–454, title IX, 

§ 906(a)(2), Oct. 13,
1978, 92 Stat. 1224.)
