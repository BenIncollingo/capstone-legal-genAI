5 U.S. Code 

§ 5407 - Nature of
payment
Any payment to an employee under this section shall be part of the employee’s
basic pay for the purposes of subchapter III of chapter 83, and chapters 84 and 87,
and for such other purposes (other than chapter 75) as the Office shall determine by
regulation.
(Added Pub. L. 108–136, div. A, title XI, 

§ 1129(a), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1645.)
