5 U.S. Code 

§ 2109 - Air traffic
controller; Secretary
For the purpose of this title—
(1) “air traffic controller” or “controller” means a civilian employee of the
Department of Transportation or the Department of Defense who, in an air traffic
control facility or flight service station facility—
(A) is actively engaged—
(i) in the separation and control of air traffic; or
