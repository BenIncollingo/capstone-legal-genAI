5 U.S. Code 

§ 3571 - Reinstatement or
restoration; individuals suspended or
removed for national security
An individual suspended or removed under section 7532 of this title may be
restored to duty in the discretion of the head of the agency concerned.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 429.)
