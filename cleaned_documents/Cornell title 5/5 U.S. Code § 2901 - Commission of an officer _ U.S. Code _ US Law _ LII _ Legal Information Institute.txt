5 U.S. Code 

§ 2901 - Commission of an
officer
The President may make out and deliver, after adjournment of the Senate, the
commission of an officer whose appointment has been confirmed by the Senate.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 411.)
