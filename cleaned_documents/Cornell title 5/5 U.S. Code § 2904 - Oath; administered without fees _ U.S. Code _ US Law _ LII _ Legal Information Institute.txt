5 U.S. Code 

§ 2904 - Oath;
administered without fees
An employee of an Executive agency who is authorized to administer the oath of
office required by section 3331 of this title, or any other oath required by law in
connection with employment in the executive branch, may not charge or receive a fee
or pay for administering the oath.
(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 412.)
