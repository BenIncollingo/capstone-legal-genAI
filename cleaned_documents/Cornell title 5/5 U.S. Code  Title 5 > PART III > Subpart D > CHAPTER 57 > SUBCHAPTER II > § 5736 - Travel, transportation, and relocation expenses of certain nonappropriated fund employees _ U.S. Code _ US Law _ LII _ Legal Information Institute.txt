5 U.S. Code 

§ 5736 - Travel,
transportation, and relocation expenses
of certain nonappropriated fund
employees
An employee of a nonappropriated fund instrumentality of the Department of
Defense or the Coast Guard described in section 2105(c) of this title who moves,
without a break in service of more than 3 days, to a position in the Department of
Defense or the Coast Guard, respectively, may be authorized travel, transportation,
and relocation expenses and allowances under the same conditions and to the same
extent authorized by this subchapter for transferred employees.
